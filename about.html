Get to Know About Us

Carla-Coiffure is a vibrant hair salon based in Lubumbashi, DRC, dedicated to providing exceptional hairstyling services for men, women, and children. Founded in 2020, the salon combines creativity and expertise to celebrate natural African hair, offering personalized care and the latest styling trends. At Carla-Coiffure, every client is treated with passion and professionalism to ensure a unique and memorable hair care experience.

Our Expertise

We offer high-quality hairstyling services for men, women, and children, celebrating natural African hair.

Our Approach

We combine creativity, skill, and passion to ensure every client leaves feeling confident and happy.

Client Experience

Every visit is personalized and memorable, providing a comfortable, professional environment.

Vision

Our vision is to grow Carla-Coiffure into a leading brand that not only serves clients locally but also exports premium hair care products internationally.

Mission

Our mission is to provide an exceptional hair care experience for everyone, using high-quality products and professional techniques to help clients embrace their natural beauty.

Our Team

Stylists & Hair Experts, Customer Care Specialists, Product & Sales Team, and Support Staff collaborate to ensure an outstanding experience for all clients.

Why We Stand Out

Our team is passionate about helping clients embrace their natural beauty in a supportive, creative environment.
